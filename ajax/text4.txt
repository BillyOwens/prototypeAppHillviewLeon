Robert Lewandowski
Footballer
Robert Lewandowski is a Polish footballer who plays as striker for Bundesliga club Bayern Munich and captains the Polish national team. Wikipedia
Born: August 21, 1988 (age 26), Warsaw, Poland
Height: 1.84 m
Spouse: Anna Stachurska (m. 2013)
Salary: 9 million EUR (2014)
Current teams: Poland national football team (Forward), FC Bayern Munich (#9 / Forward)
Parents: Iwona Lewandowska, Krzysztof Lewandowski
